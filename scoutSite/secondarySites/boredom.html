Things to do while quarntined

So you've grown bored of staying home for weeks on end, and have turned to complaining on social media about how you're losing your mind and developing a case of dissassociative identity disorder. (I'm not kidding. People in my school are doing this.)


The key to not "losing your sanity" or developing a self-diagnosed mental illness for internet pity points is to keep yourself busy.

Simply, just finding things to do around the house will alleviate the boredom. Cleaning will keep you occupied for hours on end, and the end result is a more open area for you to spend your months-long quarantine in.

Moreso, you're living in a time where technology and electronic communication is taking tremendous strides, and you're telling me that you're BORED? You literally have thousands of Triple A titles to choose from! You don't even have to leave your house to get them! Just download digitally on Steam, Playstation or what ever you use!
